UCM SHUTTLE INFORMATION

The university considered the shuttle bus service as a way to improve transportation for a growing number of UCM students who are traveling to the UCM Summit Center to participate in programs such as computer science, computer information systems, and information technology. Many of these riders are international students who do not have personal vehicles.
